Welcome to the AIBridge Bootcamp's Homepage

The purpose of the AIBridge bootcamp is to bridge the gap between AI and application domains. AIBridge will teach basic Python programming, the intuition behind machine learning, and how to use it. This bootcamp will be in-person and will target mainly non-CS people interested in AI without much prior programming experience.

Key Aspects of AIBridge

  • Provide an entry point for people who are interested in AI but without CS background.
  • Targeting non-CS adults and teens with a focus on intuition and usage of AI toolboxes.
  • Hands-on experience with immediate feedback from teachers.
  • First bootcamp offered at UC Davis in June 2022, to 17 undergraduate, graduate students, and postdocs.
  • Upcoming classes at Saratoga Library.
  • Free of charge.
