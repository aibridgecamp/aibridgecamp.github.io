AIBridge:
  • We bridge the gap between AI and application domains.

  • Upcoming training camp at Saratoga Library.

  • One summer camp will be offered at UC Davis in the summer 2023. Additional camps in other places are being planned.

Teacher Qualifications:

  • High school juniors and above, and exceptional sophomores
  • 2+ year of Python programming
  • Passion and experience in AI applications
  • Strong public speaking skills
  • Attend in-person training at Saratoga Library, California: 3/11, 3/12, 3/18, 3/19: 1pm-5pm PST
  • Commitment to attend 20 hours of training time (total)
  • Mastery of materials, lectures, and labs.
  • Prior teaching experience preferred
  • Teaching such a class takes a fair amount of time and commitment. Please consider your availability seriously.

TA Qualifications:

  • High school sophomore and above, and exceptional freshman.
  • 1+ year of Python programming.
  • Interest in AI
  • Strong communication skills
  • Attend in-person training at Saratoga Library, California: 3/11, 3/12, 3/18, 3/19: 1pm-5pm PST
  • Commitment to attend 5 hours of training time (total).
  • Full understanding of materials and completing labs.
  • Reliability, responsibility, and team-player.
