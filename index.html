Welcome to the AIBridge Bootcamp's Homepage

The purpose of AIBridge is to bridge the gap between computer science and other disciplines. To many, working with AI might seem like an unreachable objective. However, in reality, one week is enough to get started. AIBridge will provide basic programming capability in Python and knowledge of object-oriented programming as well as the concepts behind machine learning and how to implement it using a popular toolbox, Scikit-Learn.

Key Aspects of AIBridge

  • Provide Entry point for people who are interested in AI but without CS background.
  • Targeting non-CS adults and teens with a focus on intuition and usage of AI toolboxes.
  • Hands-on experience with immediate feedback from teachers.
  • First bootcamp offered at UC Davis in June 2022, to 17 undergraduate, graduate students, and postdocs.
  • Upcoming classes at Saratoga Library.
  • Free of charge.
